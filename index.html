📋 Interactive Rules

1. Budget & Bidding

Start with ₹15.00 Crore. Each bid increases price by ₹5 Lakh. You must reserve ₹10 Lakh for every empty squad slot.

2. Squad Limits

Total 18 players required: 3 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 5 FWD. Once you reach a limit, you can't bid on that position.

3. Live Timer

When a bid is placed, the timer resets to 10 seconds. If it hits zero, the player is SOLD to the highest bidder.
